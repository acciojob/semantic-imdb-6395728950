Jannat (2008) is a Bollywood crime-romance film starring Emraan Hashmi and Sonal Chauhan. The story follows Arjun, a small-time gambler who rises to success in the world of cricket betting and match-fixing. As he gains wealth and power, his relationship with his love interest, Zoya, becomes strained. His illegal dealings eventually attract the attention of law enforcement, leading to a tragic downfall. The film explores themes of greed, ambition, and love, with a gripping narrative and a memorable soundtrack.
